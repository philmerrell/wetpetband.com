Wet Pet's Hot Radio Boise Picks 2022

Apple Music Playlist Spotify Playlist

Featuring selected tracks from Antler Crafts, Country Jesus, The Daft Manifesto, Dusky Durango’s Caravan, Guest DJ Jeremy, Sonic Mushroom Cloud. Our favorite albums of the year were heard on Necessary Luxuries, Hot Potato, V3, Tennis Court Disco, The Wreck, and Forces of Obvious Luck.

 Antler Crafts

MJ Lenderman Hangover Game

I don’t typically gravitate to the alt country genre, but I’m drawn to Lenderman’s lyrics, sense of melody and lackadaisical vocal delivery.

Delicate Steve I Can Fly Away

Delicate Steve comes up with such chill vibes. Expressive guitar to an extent that lyrics are not necessary.

Blood Lemon Bruise

Three amazing musicians (and people). This song is so good. A lyrically thoughtful and riff heavy album. While I continue to enjoy this album, I can’t wait to hear what’s next.

Kikagaku Moyo Cardboard Pile

The Japanese psychedelic band that dreams are made of. Clearly influenced by Krautrock of the 70s (CAN, Neu! Popol Vuh, Amon Duul II). This song has two distinct halves, a raucous intro that smashes into an incredible drum and bass driven groove.

 Country Jesus

The French Tips Farewell

Too many amazing things to say about this incredible three piece. Incredible song writers. Two incredible albums. One of Boise’s treasures IMHO. I might be biased ;)

Floating Witch's Head Body Snatchers

I’ve always been a huge Travis Ward fan, but hearing his songwriting in the context of a psych rock band backed by an all star key and drum duo has been a real treat. If this is just a demo, we are in for a real treat from this three piece.

New Candys Vyvyan Rising

In addition to listening on the fantastic Country Jesus, I also happened upon this band at Treefort. This Italian band gives me The Brian Jonestown Massacre vibe.

Automatic Too Much Money

This song is an undeniable synth punk banger.

The Daft Manifesto

Cola Blank Curtain

I love the production of this melodic post punk song. I’m a sucker for a good vocal melody set to wry poetry. What the hell does it mean? I don't know. If the word play is interesting, it doesn’t matter to me.

Ducks Ltd. 18 Cigarettes

This jangly pop number could have easily been released in the 80s. It’s hard not to feel good listening to it.

Holiday Ghosts Off Grid

Undeniably catchy garage pop. For me it was love at first listen.

The Stroppies The Perfect Crime

This song and The Stroppies in general, didn’t strike me at first but upon repeat listens via The Daft Manifesto, I am a fan. So much so I bought it on Vinyl.

 Dusky Durango's Caravan

The Minders Hand Me Downs

I didn’t happen upon The Minders the first time around in the late 90s, but this lo-fi take on jaunty garage pop is quite nice.

Cobra Man Bad Feeling

On constant repeat for a certain French Tip I happen to know.

Beak> Ah Yeh

Drum beats that would have made Jaki Liebezeit of CAN give a tip of the cap.

MJ Lenderman Taste Just Like it Costs

Major Neil Young (ala Everybody Knows this is Nowhere) vibes on this one.

 Guest DJ Jeremy

Dear Nora This Will Be Our Year

Fantastic lo-fi cover of the original Zombies tune.

Link Wray Fire and Brimstone

As lore would have it, Link Wray created the first ever distorted guitar sound by physically punching holes in the speaker cone of his amp with a pencil on the legendary song Rumble, but this pivot to a folksy, country sound was a side of his music I hadn’t been exposed to.

Built to Spill Elements

One of my favorites on the new Built to Spill album. Amazing keyboard sounds and melody.

Oruã Obrei Orei

My favorite Brazilian band that’s helped record a Built to Spill album ever.

 Sonic Mushroom Cloud

Rodeo Screams Big Black Hearse

I stand by my words that Rodeo Screams sounds like zombie George Straight on a bad acid trip in our soon to be post apocalyptic hellscape.

The Amps Tipp City

While The Breeders were on hiatus, this Kim Deal band put out some killer tunes.

The French Tips Pretty Killer

A terrifying track about femicide.

Suzi Moon I'm Not a Man

Classic Punk AF attitude.
